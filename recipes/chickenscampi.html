Chicken Scampi

The first time I had chicken scampi at Olive Garden, I fell in love with it. I wanted to learn how to make it myself and found the perfect recipe! While this isn't the easiest or quickest dish to make, it is definitely worth the effort. Try this garlicky, creamy sauce over noodles with sliced bell peppers and onions, and I'm sure you'll instantly fall in love, too!

Ingredients

  • 8oz angel hair pasta
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 red pepper, sliced thin
  • 1 yellow pepper, sliced thin
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced thin
  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 6 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3/4 cup white wine
  • 1 1/3 cups chicken broth
  • 2/3 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions

  1. Cook the angel hair pasta according to directions and set aside.
  2. Slice chicken into strips.
  3. In a large bowl, combine flour with italian seasoning. Dredge the chicken.
  4. Heat some oil in a pan and cook chicken until browned, about 2 minutes per side. Set aside.
  5. Heat more oil in the same pan and add peppers and onion, saute for 2 minutes.
  6. Add butter and garlic, stirring until butter is melted.
  7. Pour in the wine and chicken broth, simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Remove from heat and allow to cool slightly. Add heavy whipping cream and some salt, pepper, and italian seasoning.
  9. Add pasta and chicken back into the pan and stir.
  10. Enjoy!
