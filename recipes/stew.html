Beef Stew

Description

Beef stew is so delicious and hearty, and also so easy to make! For maximum convenience, I like to use a crockpot. Toss everything into the crockpot in the morning, and by the time you come home from work, you'll have a wonderful meal waiting for you. I suggest serving it with some bread or rolls!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound stewing meat
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 5-6 medium potatoes
  • 2 large carrots
  • 1/2 sweet onion
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 sprig of thyme
  • 1 sprig of rosemary
  • 1 sprig of sage
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • splash of red wine
  • Beef broth

Directions

  1. Dice the potatoes, carrots, and onion.
  2. Dredge the meat in the flour.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and sear the meat until it is browned on all sides. Put meat into a large crockpot.
  4. Heat more oil in the pan and saute onion until transluscent.
  5. Add onion, potatoes, carrots, garlic, herbs, tomato paste, and wine to the crockpot.
  6. Add salt to taste
  7. Add beef broth until other ingredients are covered.
  8. Cook on low for 6-8 hours.
  9. Remove springs and bay leaf and serve stew with rolls!
